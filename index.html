  • Biography

    I am educated in architecture at The Cooper Union but I spent most of my career as a fashion designer of the NYC based contemporary designer brand United Bamboo.          

    In 2012, United Bamboo was sold to our Japanese financiers and so I ventured into the tech world  as an entrepreneur in a design role at various startups. 

    Nowadays I define myself as a UX designer. I am currently working with NYC.gov Information Technology Services as Lead Product Designer designing an enterprise web app for Human Resources Administration.

  • Design phiiosophy

    I would describe my design philosophy as a hybridization of Design Thinking and Modernism 

    I was profoundly influenced by Bauhaus era Modernism especially as it relates to experimenting with new frontiers in design.  While at the same time, I believe great design should offer elegant solutions to complex and messy problems.  

    In UX, I practice Human Centered Design with great emphasis on users and I find myself arguing for the users in design meetings.
