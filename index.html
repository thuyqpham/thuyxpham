  • Biography

    I studied architecture at The Cooper Union, but the majority of my career was spent as a fashion designer with the iconic New York brand United Bamboo. In 2012, after United Bamboo was sold to our Japanese investors, I pivoted into the tech world, taking on design-focused entrepreneurial roles at various startups.

    Today, I identify as a UX designer. Most recently, I served as Senior Product Designer at Carrier Corporation, where I contributed to the design of Lynx Fleet, a B2B web and mobile application for IoT-enabled vehicle fleet management, and the Air Design System that is used company wide for all Carrier Products.

    Currently I am exploring the possibilities integration of AI use into the UX & Design pipeline.

  • Design phiiosophy

    My design philosophy blends Design Thinking with Modernism—particularly the Bauhaus ethos, which deeply shaped my approach to exploring new frontiers in design. At the same time, I believe great design lies in delivering elegant solutions to complex, often messy problems.
