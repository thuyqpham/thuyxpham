Designer brand from Italy

UX & UI design
project brief

Client is a Italian high-end designer brand that is launching e-commerce website. The company recently received funding from Italian apparel group wna dis looking to re-lauch with updated branding and website.

design requirements

It was important to the client that the design reflect the uniqueness of the brand identity. Some adjectives given were: modern, quirky, architectural. 

Mockups of typography and white space were sent to client for approval before UX design.  Sparse use of icons mimic print design and injection of manipulated content inspired by VHS stills adds playfulness to an otherwise rigid design. Project objectives:

  • Convey brand identity
  • Unique and creative design
  • UI micro interactions to elevate experience
