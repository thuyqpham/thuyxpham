Social App for stock traders. Built on Android

Lead Product Design

A social trading app called 10Bagger—a dynamic platform where retail investors connect, collaborate, and learn from one another in real time. Inspired by Facebook’s news feed and community groups, 10Bagger features a personalized feed that surfaces posts from traders you follow, trending stock discussions, and real-time market commentary. Users can create or join “Trading Circles” (akin to Facebook Groups) centered around specific strategies—like swing trading, dividend growth, or crypto volatility—enabling niche communities to share insights, charts, and alerts. Every post supports rich media, including annotated price charts, embedded earnings reports, and sentiment heatmaps, turning casual scrolling into actionable intelligence.

At its core, 10Bagger integrates seamless trading functionality reminiscent of Robinhood: with one tap, users can execute trades directly from a peer’s shared idea or from their own watchlist. If a respected trader in your Circle posts, “Just added NVDA ahead of earnings—risk managed with a stop at $120,” followers can view the full trade rationale, risk parameters, and even replicate the position instantly through an integrated brokerage API. The app also includes a “Trade Mirror” feature, allowing less experienced users to auto-sync trades from trusted mentors—similar to how TikTok users follow creators—but with full transparency on portfolio impact and historical performance.

“At its core, 10Bagger integrates seamless trading functionality reminiscent of Robinhood”

To foster accountability and learning, 10Bagger gamifies transparency: every published trade is automatically tracked, and users earn reputation badges based on consistency, risk-adjusted returns, and community engagement—not just profitability. Think Duolingo streaks meets LinkedIn endorsements, but for trading discipline. Privacy remains customizable—you can share your full portfolio, anonymized performance stats, or stay lurker-mode while absorbing insights. By blending the social connectivity of mainstream platforms with the real-time action of trading apps, 10Bagger transforms solitary screen-staring into a collaborative, human-centered investing experience.
