North star project for jewelry brand

UX & UI design

Client is a global jewelry brand looking to redesign of existing e-commerce experience.  Client sent market research report that included data on industry trends, user demographics and shopping behavior.  We also conducted usability studies using usertesting.com to analyze pain points experienced by users.  Design was to knowledge gained from these studies

some stand out issues

Male customers need help from sales staff in order to close the sale.  Client wanted to bring this in store experience online via "gift finder" feature which was not being noticed by user.

Client wanted to integrate of online and in store  as a seamless experience.  New feature to be designed that allows customers to create wishlist of items then make appointment with sales staff to see items in store.

Studies from usertesting.com found user experienced an identity disconnect between visual identity and brand identity.  Visual design overhaul with style, color and typography in order to harmonize with brand identity.

Client wanted to expose & promote community of Pandora enthusiasts that exist outside of website on social media such as blogs or Instagram.

redesign and new features
  • Gift finder feature
  • Integration with in store experience
  • Visual identity re-design
  • Integration with Instagram
