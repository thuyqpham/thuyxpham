Browns Shoes

  1. home
  2. projects
  3. browns shoes

E-commerce website for large fashion shoes retail chain.

UX & UI design
design exploration

Client is a large Canadian shoe retail company with over 65 brick & mortar stores.  The client feels the current site looks outdated and wanted a desgin facelift.

redesign

Prior to design phase UX team conducted workshop with client stakeholders. During workshop card sorting excercise was conducted along with some research into analytics for user behavior. Objectives for this project:

  • Elevate visual design to match branding as trendy contempory fashion image
  • Improve navigation through re-organization of sitemap
  • Create modular design system for versatility of seasonal needs
tools used

Sketch used for design & wireframes. Invision used for prototyping.
